Wings of Hope Fundraising Events in 2019

Wings of Hope Breast Cancer Foundation are pleased to announce the 2019 fundraising events. Please join us in celebrating our annual luncheon or any of the upcoming events so we can continue to support our clients. Our clients rely on the hard work of our volunteers, as well as the gracious donations you make. 97 percent of the funds we raise goes directly to our clients, low-income individuals living with breast cancer undergoing treatments in the Tom Bake Cancer Center.

How you can help

Treatments can last for 6-12 month. In these trying months, patients often can’t work and our clients won’t receive any health benefits, or disability payments either. Their ability to only focus on getting better is impaired, often struggling to make rent or providing groceries as well as being able to afford transportation to treatments or childcare while they undergo treatments. When you make a donation, you are helping with housing needs or put food on the table for a family struggling with breast cancer.
